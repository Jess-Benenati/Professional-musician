My Portfolio

Here are just a couple of examples of projects I have worked on in the past.

Bring Me To Life (Evanescence Cover)

Eminescence was formed in 2022 to become New York's only Evanescence cover band, playing all the classics and beyond from the band.

This video was released in 2023, and featured Emi Pelegrino on vocals, Tom Schinkel on bass, Nick Bavaro on drums, and Jonathan Gunter on guitar. I was the featured piano player in the video and on the recording.

Night Witches by Sharp Violet

Formed in New York in 2016, Sharp Violet is a Riot Grrl punk band focusing on original songs written by all members of the band.

This video was released in 2022 and was made to represent the 588th Night Bomber Regiment during World War 2. This regiment, nicknamed "Night Witches," consisted of all female piolets on a mission to help take down the Nazis.

This video features Liz on vocals, Alli on rhythm guitar, Carolyn on drums, and myself on lead guitar.

My Influences

Here are some musicians that made me who I am today.

Ludwig van Beethoven

I do gravitate towards classical music on piano. That is because of Beethoven's music. Knowing that his music influenced so many classical musicians is very inspiring.

Freddie Mercury

Freddie Mercury's piano playing showed me that piano playing doesn't have to take away from the intencity of a rock song. It should add to it, when it's done right.

Billie Joe Armstrong

Growing up in the 90's, Green Day was a big part of punk rock music and is still doing it today. I was attracted to Billie Joe's guitar playing for his simple but effective way of playing and writing. He is also not afraid to try new things to evolve the genre.

Daron Malakian

System of a Down will always be one of my favorite bands for taking stances against what's wrong and for making music that sounds like no other music. Daron is a big part of why they sound so different with his guitar playing and writing style, where he incorporates fast paced riffs and dancable groves thoughout all of their songs.
